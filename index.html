Boggle - A Word Search Game

How to Play

  • Click START and start searching! You have 3 minutes.
  • Click on adjacent letters to build words of 3 letters or more.
  • Press ENTER to submit each word
  • 3 and 4 letter words are worth 1 point. Get 1 point for each additional letter
  • Score 25 points for gold, 20 for silver and 15 for bronze.
