Boggle - A Word Search Game

How to Play

  • Choose a time limit and get ready to start searching
  • Click on adjacent letters to build words of 3 or more letters
  • Press ENTER to submit each word
  • 3 letter words are worth 10 points. Get 5 points for each additional letter
  • Score at least X points to win
